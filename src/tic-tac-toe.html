Tic-Tac-Toe

The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row. The first player is known as X
and the second is O. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the game board until
either oppent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled. X always goes first,
and in the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a tie game.
